Destination: Thailand

Overview:
Thailand is one of the most affordable and popular international destinations for Indian travelers.

Popular Attractions:
- Bangkok
- Pattaya
- Phuket
- Krabi
- Phi Phi Islands

Best Time to Visit:
November to March

Recommended Duration:
5 to 8 Days

Ideal For:
- Families
- Couples
- Friends
- Solo Travelers

Activities:
- Island Hopping
- Shopping
- Water Sports
- Cultural Tours
- Night Markets

Visa Information:
Travelers should verify the latest visa requirements before travel. Passport validity should generally be at least six months.

Package Information:
Packages usually include hotels, airport transfers, sightseeing tours, and breakfast.

Budget Range:
₹50,000 to ₹1,20,000 per person

FAQ:

Q: Is Thailand good for first-time international travelers?
A: Yes, Thailand is often recommended due to affordability and tourist-friendly infrastructure.
