Destination: Bali, Indonesia

Overview:
Bali is one of the most popular international destinations for Indian travelers. It is known for beaches, temples, cultural experiences, luxury resorts, and adventure activities.

Popular Attractions:
- Uluwatu Temple
- Tanah Lot Temple
- Nusa Penida Island
- Kuta Beach
- Seminyak
- Ubud Monkey Forest
- Tegallalang Rice Terraces

Best Time to Visit:
April to October

Recommended Duration:
5 to 7 Days

Ideal For:
- Families
- Honeymoon Couples
- Adventure Travelers
- Group Trips

Popular Activities:
- Water Sports
- ATV Rides
- Snorkeling
- Temple Tours
- Island Tours
- Cultural Performances

Visa Information:
Travelers should verify the latest visa requirements before travel. Passport validity should generally be at least six months.

Package Information:
Typical Bali packages include hotel accommodation, breakfast, airport transfers, sightseeing tours, and local transportation.

Budget Range:
₹60,000 to ₹1,50,000 per person

Frequently Asked Questions:

Q: Is Bali suitable for families?
A: Yes, Bali is a family-friendly destination with beaches, safaris, water parks, and cultural attractions.

Q: How many days are enough for Bali?
A: Most travelers prefer 5 to 7 days.
